This website is my little reclaimed corner of the real and actual fun internet. That is, as a collective expression of information of various forms, linked together with <a> tags.

The internet has for a long time been strongly moving towards watered down, corporate friendly, and overly "social" spaces that are anathema to creative expression and individuality. I have contributed to this atmosphere in a variety of ways, and have largely forgotten what the internet actually is. Or what it used to be. Or what it has the potential to be.

There is plenty of good writing on this topic (such as The rise of Whatever) so here I'll just stick to my contributions. I plan to add a few posts about interesting things I'm up to in this internet reclamation sphere. Right now I'm interested in self-hosting some stuff, especially by resurrecting old devices and repurposing them for semi-functional and semi-artistic uses.
